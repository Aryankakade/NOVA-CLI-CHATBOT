What is NOVA-AI?

NOVA-AI is a next-generation artificial intelligence assistant designed to help you with coding, business, career development, and personal growth. It combines state-of-the-art language models with specialized domain knowledge to provide accurate and helpful responses.

Unlike traditional chatbots, NOVA adapts to your needs, remembers your preferences, and grows with you over time. Whether you're debugging code, planning a business strategy, or just need someone to talk to, NOVA is here to help.
